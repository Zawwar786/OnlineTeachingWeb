Information Security

Information Security is a course that focuses on the protection of sensitive information and systems from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction. The course covers a range of topics, including risk management, security controls, security architecture and design, legal and regulatory compliance, and incident response.

Information Security courses are geared toward computer science, information technology, and other technical majors, but can also be suitable for non-technical students who are interested in understanding how to protect information and systems.

Enterprise Engineering

Enterprise Engineering is a multidisciplinary field that focuses on the design, implementation, and management of an enterprise, which is typically defined as a large organization or a complex system of systems. It encompasses a wide range of disciplines, including systems engineering, information technology, business management, and organizational behavior.

The goal of enterprise engineering is to optimize the overall performance of an enterprise by aligning its technical and organizational components in a way that supports the achievement of its strategic goals and objectives.

Principle of Management

Principles of Management is the study of the fundamental practices and techniques used by managers to lead and direct an organization. It covers a wide range of topics, including planning, organizing, staffing, leading, and controlling.

The goal of the Principles of management is to help students understand the key concepts and techniques used by managers to lead and direct organizations, and to develop the skills needed to become effective managers themselves.

Software Quality

Software Quality is a field of study that focuses on the methods, tools, and techniques used to ensure that software systems are fit for their intended purpose and meet the needs of their stakeholders.

The goal of SQE courses is to provide students with a deep understanding of the software development life cycle, as well as the tools and techniques used to evaluate and improve software quality. The course also aims to provide students with hands-on experience in the use of industry-standard tools and technologies used to measure and improve software quality.

Web Engineering

Web Engineering is a multidisciplinary field that focuses on the systematic and efficient development of web-based systems and applications. It encompasses a wide range of disciplines, including web design, web development, web architecture, and web management.

Web engineering plays a vital role in today's world as the internet and web-based technology have become an integral part of our daily lives and it's becoming a vital part of many industries such as e-commerce, e-learning, banking and many more.
